Methodology (GIFs here currently are only placeholders)

Navigation World Model (NWM) Setup

The Navigation World Model processes visual inputs and trajectory data to learn spatial representations for navigation tasks.

Our Fine-tuning Approach

Familiar Scenes

Training on environments the model has previously encountered to reinforce spatial understanding.

Half-Familiar Scenes

Combining known spatial layouts with novel visual elements to improve generalization.

Unfamiliar Scenes

Testing on completely novel environments to evaluate transfer learning capabilities.
